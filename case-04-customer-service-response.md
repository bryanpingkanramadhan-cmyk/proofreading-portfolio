# Case 04 — Customer Service Response Review

## Overview

This case demonstrates the proofreading and linguistic quality assurance process for a customer service response. The objective is to improve clarity, professionalism, empathy, grammar, punctuation, and consistency while preserving the original intent of the response.

---

## Document Type

Customer Service Response

---

## Target Audience

Customers

---

## Objective

To deliver a professional and empathetic response to a customer who reports that a parcel has not arrived within the estimated delivery time.

---

# Original Document

**Customer Inquiry**

Halo paket saya belum sampai padahal estimasi pengiriman sudah lewat dua hari saya butuh paket tersebut secepatnya tolong segera dicek.

**Customer Service Response**

Mohon maaf atas keterlambatan paket anda kami akan cek terlebih dahulu apabila sudah ada informasi nanti kami kabari kembali terima kasih.

---

# Identified Issues

| Category        | Description                                                               |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Grammar         | Run-on sentence without logical separation                                |
| Punctuation     | Missing commas, periods, and paragraph spacing                            |
| Capitalization  | Incorrect capitalization of formal pronouns ("Anda")                      |
| Tone            | Response lacks empathy and reassurance                                    |
| Clarity         | No explanation of the next steps or expected follow-up                    |
| Professionalism | Closing message is too brief and does not encourage further communication |

---

# Risk Assessment

Potential risks if the response is sent without revision:

* Customers may feel their concerns are not taken seriously.
* The response does not provide clear expectations regarding follow-up.
* The company may appear unprofessional or unresponsive.
* Customer satisfaction and trust may decrease.

---

# Proofread Version

**Customer Inquiry**

Halo, paket saya belum sampai, padahal estimasi pengiriman sudah lewat dua hari. Saya membutuhkan paket tersebut secepatnya. Mohon bantuannya untuk melakukan pengecekan.

---

**Revised Customer Service Response**

Halo, Bapak/Ibu.

Terima kasih telah menghubungi layanan pelanggan kami.

Kami mohon maaf atas keterlambatan pengiriman paket Anda. Kami memahami bahwa paket tersebut penting bagi Anda dan kami turut menyesalkan ketidaknyamanan yang terjadi.

Saat ini kami sedang melakukan pengecekan terhadap status pengiriman paket Anda melalui sistem pelacakan internal. Setelah memperoleh informasi terbaru, kami akan segera menghubungi Anda kembali.

Apabila diperlukan, kami juga akan berkoordinasi dengan kantor tujuan maupun petugas pengiriman untuk mempercepat proses penanganan.

Terima kasih atas kesabaran dan pengertian Anda.

Hormat kami,

Customer Service

---

# Summary of Changes

## Grammar

* Separated one long response into multiple logical paragraphs.
* Improved sentence structure for better readability.

---

## Punctuation

Added:

* Full stops.
* Commas.
* Greeting format.
* Proper paragraph spacing.

---

## Capitalization

Corrected:

* anda → **Anda**
* paket anda → **paket Anda**

Applied formal Indonesian business writing conventions.

---

## Tone Improvement

Original tone:

* Generic apology.
* Minimal reassurance.

Improved tone:

* Expresses empathy.
* Acknowledges customer concerns.
* Explains actions being taken.
* Provides reassurance and clear expectations.

---

## Clarity

Added information regarding:

* Investigation process.
* Internal tracking system.
* Follow-up commitment.
* Coordination with operational teams if necessary.

These additions help reduce uncertainty and improve customer confidence.

---

## Professionalism

The revised response adopts a customer-centric communication style by:

* Greeting the customer politely.
* Thanking the customer for contacting support.
* Explaining the issue transparently.
* Providing a clear action plan.
* Closing with appreciation and professionalism.

---

# Before vs After

## After

# Key Takeaways

Proofreading customer service content requires more than correcting grammar. A high-quality response should also demonstrate empathy, provide clear information, manage customer expectations, and maintain a professional tone. These elements contribute to better customer satisfaction and are essential for language quality evaluation in AI-assisted customer support systems.
