# Case 03 — Business Email Review

## Overview

This case demonstrates the proofreading and quality assurance process for a business email. The objective is to improve professionalism, readability, grammar, punctuation, tone, and overall communication effectiveness while preserving the original intent.

---

## Document Type

Business Email

---

## Target Audience

Customers

---

## Objective

To communicate a shipment delay professionally while maintaining customer trust and providing clear next steps.

---

# Original Document

**Subject:** Keterlambatan Pengiriman Paket

Yth pelanggan,

Mohon maaf paket anda mengalami keterlambatan karena adanya kendala operasional kami sedang berusaha agar paket segera dikirim apabila sudah diterima kurir akan segera menghubungi anda terima kasih.

---

# Identified Issues

| Category       | Description                                       |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Grammar        | Several run-on sentences reduce readability       |
| Punctuation    | Missing commas, periods, and paragraph separation |
| Capitalization | Incorrect capitalization of formal pronouns       |
| Tone           | Apology lacks empathy and reassurance             |
| Clarity        | Cause and next steps are not clearly explained    |
| Formatting     | Business email structure is incomplete            |

---

# Risk Assessment

Potential risks if the email is sent without revision:

* Customers may perceive the company as unprofessional.
* The message may create confusion about shipment status.
* Lack of reassurance may reduce customer confidence.
* Increased likelihood of follow-up complaints due to unclear information.

---

# Proofread Version

**Subject:** Informasi Keterlambatan Pengiriman Paket

Yth. Pelanggan,

Terima kasih telah menggunakan layanan kami.

Kami mohon maaf atas keterlambatan pengiriman paket Anda. Keterlambatan ini disebabkan oleh kendala operasional yang sedang kami tangani.

Tim kami sedang berupaya agar paket Anda dapat segera diproses dan dikirim. Setelah paket diserahkan kepada kurir, informasi pelacakan akan diperbarui dan proses pengiriman akan dilanjutkan sesuai prosedur.

Kami memahami bahwa ketepatan waktu pengiriman sangat penting bagi Anda. Oleh karena itu, kami terus melakukan pemantauan hingga proses pengiriman selesai.

Apabila Anda memerlukan informasi lebih lanjut, silakan menghubungi layanan pelanggan kami.

Terima kasih atas pengertian dan kepercayaan Anda.

Hormat kami,

Tim Layanan Pelanggan

---

# Summary of Changes

## Grammar

* Split one long paragraph into several logical paragraphs.
* Improved sentence flow and coherence.

---

## Punctuation

Added:

* Full stops
* Commas
* Proper paragraph separation

Improved readability significantly.

---

## Capitalization

Corrected:

* anda → **Anda**
* pelanggan → **Pelanggan** (in salutation)

Reason:

Formal Indonesian business communication requires respectful capitalization for pronouns and salutations.

---

## Tone Improvement

Original tone:

Short apology without reassurance.

Improved tone:

* Shows empathy.
* Explains the situation.
* Describes actions being taken.
* Invites further communication.
* Ends with appreciation.

---

## Clarity

Added information regarding:

* Cause of delay.
* Current operational status.
* Expected process.
* Customer support channel.

These additions reduce ambiguity while maintaining transparency.

---

## Formatting

The revised email follows a standard business structure:

* Subject
* Greeting
* Opening
* Explanation
* Resolution
* Customer support
* Closing
* Signature

---

# Key Takeaways

Effective proofreading involves more than correcting grammar and spelling. It also requires evaluating tone, structure, clarity, audience expectations, and communication objectives. In customer-facing communication, improving these elements helps strengthen trust, reduce misunderstanding, and deliver a more professional experience.
