# Case 02 — Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Review

## Overview

This case demonstrates the proofreading and quality assurance process for a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP). The objective is to improve clarity, consistency, readability, and compliance with professional documentation standards while preserving the original operational intent.

---

## Document Type

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

---

## Target Audience

Frontline Officers

---

## Objective

To standardize the procedure for accepting customer parcels at a service counter and ensure that all employees follow the same operational steps.

---

# Original Document

## SOP: Penerimaan Paket

Petugas menerima paket dari pelanggan kemudian memeriksa paket tersebut apakah layak dikirim atau tidak lalu menimbang paket menginput data kedalam sistem kemudian memberitahu biaya kirim kepada pelanggan setelah pelanggan melakukan pembayaran cetak resi dan serahkan kepada pelanggan lalu tempel label pada paket kemudian paket dipindahkan ke area sortir.

---

# Identified Issues

| Category    | Description                                     |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Grammar     | Long run-on sentence without logical separation |
| Punctuation | Missing commas and full stops                   |
| Word Choice | Informal and inconsistent wording               |
| Formatting  | No numbered procedure                           |
| Terminology | Inconsistent operational terminology            |
| Readability | Difficult to follow sequential actions          |

---

# Risk Assessment

Potential operational risks if the document is used without revision:

* Employees may perform steps in the wrong order.
* Important verification activities may be skipped.
* Training becomes inconsistent across branches.
* Different interpretations may reduce service quality.

---

# Proofread & Standardized Version

# Standard Operating Procedure

## Parcel Acceptance at Service Counter

### Purpose

To ensure every parcel acceptance process is carried out accurately, consistently, and in accordance with operational standards.

### Procedure

1. Receive the parcel from the customer.
2. Inspect the parcel to ensure it complies with applicable shipping requirements.
3. Measure and weigh the parcel.
4. Enter shipment information into the system.
5. Confirm the shipment details with the customer.
6. Inform the customer of the shipping cost.
7. Receive payment using the available payment method.
8. Print the shipment receipt.
9. Hand the receipt to the customer.
10. Attach the shipment label securely to the parcel.
11. Move the parcel to the designated sorting area.

---

# Summary of Changes

## Grammar

* Divided one long sentence into eleven procedural steps.
* Improved sentence structure.

---

## Punctuation

Added:

* Full stops.
* Headings.
* Ordered numbering.

---

## Formatting

Changed from:

Continuous paragraph.

To:

Structured SOP with:

* Title
* Purpose
* Procedure
* Numbered steps

---

## Terminology Standardization

| Original       | Revised                                    |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| menginput data | enter shipment information into the system |
| biaya kirim    | shipping cost                              |
| cetak resi     | print the shipment receipt                 |
| area sortir    | designated sorting area                    |

Reason:

The revised terminology is more consistent with formal operational documentation.

---

## Readability

The revised SOP allows employees to:

* Follow each task sequentially.
* Quickly locate a specific step.
* Reduce interpretation errors.

---

# Before vs After

## After

* Logical workflow
* Easy to train new employees
