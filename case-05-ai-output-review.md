# Case 05 — AI Output Review

## Overview

This case demonstrates the linguistic quality assurance process for AI-generated content in Indonesian. The objective is to evaluate the output for grammatical accuracy, factual neutrality, clarity, fluency, consistency, and appropriateness for the intended audience.

This review simulates a typical AI evaluation task commonly performed in AI data annotation, language quality assessment, and large language model (LLM) training projects.

---

## Document Type

AI-Generated Customer Information

---

## Target Audience

General Public

---

## Objective

To assess and improve an AI-generated response before it is presented to end users.

---

# Prompt Given to the AI

**"Jelaskan bagaimana cara melacak paket yang dikirim melalui layanan pos."**

---

# Original AI Output

Untuk melacak paket anda cukup buka website kemudian masukan nomor resi lalu sistem akan langsung memperlihatkan lokasi paket secara real time dan dipastikan selalu akurat apabila tidak muncul berarti paket kemungkinan hilang sehingga pelanggan sebaiknya segera mengajukan komplain.

---

# Identified Issues

| Category        | Description                                                                 |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Grammar         | Run-on sentence and incorrect capitalization                                |
| Punctuation     | Missing commas and periods                                                  |
| Clarity         | Explanation is too brief and lacks guidance                                 |
| Accuracy        | Claims "real-time" tracking and "always accurate" without qualification     |
| Neutrality      | Assumes a missing tracking result means the parcel may be lost              |
| Tone            | Uses absolute language that may mislead users                               |
| User Experience | Does not explain alternative actions if tracking information is unavailable |

---

# Risk Assessment

Potential risks if the AI output is published without review:

* Users may receive misleading information.
* Overly absolute statements may reduce trust.
* Incorrect assumptions may increase unnecessary customer complaints.
* The response does not guide users through reasonable troubleshooting steps.

---

# Proofread & Reviewed Version

Untuk melacak paket, buka situs web atau aplikasi resmi penyedia layanan pengiriman, kemudian masukkan nomor resi pada halaman pelacakan.

Apabila data pelacakan tersedia, sistem akan menampilkan informasi mengenai status terbaru pengiriman sesuai pembaruan yang diterima. Waktu pembaruan dapat bervariasi tergantung pada proses operasional.

Jika informasi pelacakan belum muncul atau belum diperbarui, hal tersebut tidak selalu berarti paket hilang. Tunggu beberapa saat, kemudian lakukan pengecekan kembali. Apabila setelah jangka waktu yang wajar informasi masih belum tersedia, Anda dapat menghubungi layanan pelanggan untuk memperoleh bantuan lebih lanjut.

---

# Summary of Changes

## Grammar

* Split one long sentence into several logical paragraphs.
* Improved sentence structure.

---

## Punctuation

Added:

* Full stops.
* Commas.
* Paragraph separation.

Improved readability and comprehension.

---

## Accuracy Review

Original Statement

> "...sistem akan langsung memperlihatkan lokasi paket secara real time dan dipastikan selalu akurat..."

Issue

The statement presents information as universally true without qualification.

Revision

The revised version explains that tracking information depends on operational updates and avoids unsupported guarantees.

---

## Neutrality Review

Original Statement

> "...apabila tidak muncul berarti paket kemungkinan hilang..."

Issue

This conclusion is speculative and may unnecessarily alarm users.

Revision

The revised version recommends waiting for updates and contacting customer service if necessary.

---

## Tone Improvement

Original Tone

* Absolute
* Potentially alarming

Improved Tone

* Informative
* Neutral
* Customer-friendly
* Evidence-aware

---

## User Experience Review

Additional guidance was included to help users understand:

* How tracking systems generally work.
* Why tracking updates may not appear immediately.
* Appropriate next steps.

---

# Key Takeaways

Reviewing AI-generated content involves more than correcting grammar. A language reviewer must evaluate whether the response is clear, accurate, neutral, and appropriate for the intended audience. Effective AI quality assurance requires identifying unsupported claims, avoiding misleading language, improving readability, and ensuring that the final response communicates information responsibly while preserving the user's original intent.
